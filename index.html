Bio

I am a Postdoc in MBZUAI, working with Prof. Hao Li. I received my Ph.D. from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), advised by Prof. Pascal Fua. Prior to that, I obtained my M.S. from Purdue Universty in 2019, and my B.S. from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016.

I will be joining the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in July 2026.
